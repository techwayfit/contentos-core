# Architecture Decision Records (ADR)

This directory contains the Architecture Decision Records for **TechWayFit ContentOS**.

ADRs capture significant architectural decisions, their context, and consequences.
They are living documents and may be superseded over time.

## ADR Lifecycle

- **Proposed** – under discussion
- **Accepted** – approved and implemented
- **Superseded** – replaced by a newer ADR

All ADRs follow a lightweight format:
Context → Decision → Consequences.
